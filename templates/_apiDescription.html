Valid Endpoint :

/hypeor /hype/top - GET:

Will retrieve the top most popular tracks ranked this week as of today

/hype/remixes - GET:

Will retrieve the top remixes only most popular tracks ranked this week as of today

/hype/noremixes - GET:

Will retrieve the top original un-remixed tracks only most popular tracks ranked this week as of today

Parameters:

type ("popular" | "latest")

type="popular" (e.g.: /hype/noremixes?type="popular"):

This is the default behavior and will grab the most popular remixes

type="latest" (e.g.: /hype/noremixes?type="latest") :

Will retrieve latest non-remixes sorted by rank
